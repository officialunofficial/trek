Hello, we believe in building in the open and sharing progress publicly with people who care about resilient infrastructure, peer-to-peer systems, and practical tools. This invitation outlines the next phase of work and explains why we are focusing on reliability, documentation, and long-term maintainability over short-term growth.

Over the coming months, we will publish architecture notes, design tradeoffs, and implementation updates. We aim to make decisions legible, reduce operational surprises, and create a clearer path for contributors who want to understand the system deeply.
