## Important Notes When Buying Premium Apartments

### Legal Matters

Three new laws on land, housing and real estate business took effect from August 1, 2024, simplifying procedures, protecting buyer rights and enhancing transparency. However, you still need to carefully check:

- Land use certificate of the developer
- Construction permit and fire safety approval
- Ownership type: Long-term title or sale contract

### Pricing

Current price levels are clearly differentiated by location:

- **Riverside, central District 1:** 100-200+ million/sqm
- **Beachfront, District 3:** 80-150 million/sqm
- **Suburban areas:** 60-100 million/sqm

*Note: These are reference prices. Actual prices depend on view, floor, direction and policies at each time.*

#### Should you choose riverside or beachfront apartments?

Riverside apartments (District 1) are suitable for those who prioritize living near the center, convenient for work and urban amenities. Higher prices (100-200+ million/sqm) but good liquidity. Beachfront apartments (District 3) suit those who enjoy resort-style living with ocean views. Lower prices (80-150 million/sqm) with good appreciation potential as infrastructure develops.
