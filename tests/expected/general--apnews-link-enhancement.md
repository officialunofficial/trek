## Health care subsidies at risk in shutdown

WASHINGTON (AP) — The first caller on a telephone town hall with Maryland Rep. Andy Harris came ready with a question about the Affordable Care Act. Her cousin's disabled son is at risk of losing the insurance he gained [under that law](https://apnews.com/article/aca-credits-health-care-subsidies), the caller said.

The caller's question underscored the high stakes of a potential government shutdown. The [subsidies that make insurance affordable](https://apnews.com/article/aca-credits-subsidies-government-shutdown) for millions of Americans could be at risk.
