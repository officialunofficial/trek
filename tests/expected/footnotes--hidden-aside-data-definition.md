The first season split screen time between at least three major storylines. Season two said hold my coffee, giving us six threads to follow.[^1]

For the second installment, the writers stuck much closer to what was established in the source material, mostly the second entry, but with a smattering of references from the other entries.[^2]

The new faction introduced in episode three was a welcome addition to the lore.[^3]

[^1]: By my count: 1) Alice and Bob. 2) Bob's backstory. 3) Charlie and the team. 4) Drama at headquarters. 5) Diana and the organization. 6) Eve and her scheming.

[^2]: It took me a while to understand that by "the district," they meant [the Leader's](https://example.com/wiki/The_Leader) chapter.

[^3]: I'm glad to see the old guard back, but I'm skeptical they've been camped out this whole time.
