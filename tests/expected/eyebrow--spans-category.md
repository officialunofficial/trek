Our latest platform is now generally available to all customers. It represents a significant step forward in how teams collaborate and build together. We have been working toward this milestone for two years.

The new platform unifies three previously separate tools into one coherent experience. Users can now switch between editing, reviewing, and publishing without leaving the app. This change alone cuts the average task duration by a meaningful margin.

Early beta testers reported a sixty percent reduction in time spent context switching. Feedback has been overwhelmingly positive across every customer segment we work with, from solo developers to large enterprise teams.

## Built for Teams

The platform supports real-time collaboration with full audit history. Every edit is tracked, every comment threaded, and every change attributable to a specific person and moment. Teams that need this level of traceability have been asking for it for years.

Permissions can be configured at the workspace, project, or document level. Role-based access control covers the most common scenarios, and fine-grained overrides handle the rest. We designed this system after interviewing more than forty customers.

## Getting Started

Existing customers will see the new platform appear in their dashboard automatically this week. New customers can sign up through the usual onboarding flow without any additional steps. The migration happens entirely on our side.

If you have questions or feedback, reach out to our support team. We are especially interested in hearing about any friction you encounter during your first session in the new experience.
